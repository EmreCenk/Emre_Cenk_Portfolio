About

"Fine, I'll do it myself."

  • This bot was designed purely out of curiosity. I wanted a way to automate extracting statistics from my account. However, every service I found requested my password, and was extremely shady. That is when I decided to create a secure Instagram Bot that would not ask for my password. Everything used here is open source. You can even examine the source code if you want. After some time, I realized that I could deploy my bot onto a website. The rest is history. *dramatic music intensifies*
